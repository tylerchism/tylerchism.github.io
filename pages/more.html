About Me

I'm constantly in pursuit of knowledge and happiest when learning something new. I'm diligent about getting to the root of issues and doing things the right way - I don't consider myself competent in a concept until I grasp its underlying principles.

This approach has allowed me to excel across a wide range of STEM disciplines without limiting myself to one narrow field. My goal is to be a producer rather than a consumer, using my knowledge to create solutions that advance society.

I believe that while technical skills and work ethic are crucial, communication and teamwork are vital components of success. That's why I invest in developing not just my scientific knowledge, but also my abilities as a communicator and colleague.

Home Life

Home is about an hour from Fayetteville in Huntsville, AR, where the Buffalo National River flows right through our property. This spring-fed river maintains a perfect 58 degrees year-round, and our 25 acres of beautiful hardwood trees provide the perfect natural sanctuary.

I'm passionate about conservation and sustainability, spending time learning about environmental stewardship, evolutionary biology, and how humans can live more harmoniously with nature. Most of my favorite activities take me outdoors, though I also enjoy strategy-based games and deep conversations.

Health and fitness are top priorities - I'm certified as a biomechanics specialist and continuously study nutrition, physical fitness, and human optimization. Future plans for our land include expanding off-grid capabilities, creating meditation spaces, and developing it as a sustainable retreat that embodies these values.
