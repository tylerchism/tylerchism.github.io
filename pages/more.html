About Me

I'm constantly in pursuit of knowledge and happiest when learning something new. I'm diligent about getting to the root of issues and doing things the right way - I don't consider myself to have competently grasped a concept until I have internalized its underlying principles.

This approach has allowed me to excel across a wide range of STEM disciplines without limiting myself to one narrow field. My goal is to be a producer rather than a consumer, using my knowledge to create solutions that advance society.

Home Life

Home is in the Ozarks, where I live on 320 acres bordering the national forest. This land is a sanctuary for me, where I can immerse myself in nature and work on projects that align with my values.

I'm passionate about conservation and sustainability, spending time on things like: restoring native habitat, agroforestry, and permaculture. Most of my favorite activities take me outdoors, though I also enjoy strategy-based games and deep conversations.

Health and fitness are top priorities - I'm certified as a biomechanics specialist and continuously study nutrition, physical fitness, and human optimization.
